Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 29, 2025

At Family Values Group, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or communicate with us in any way.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information from you:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address
  • Family information (such as spouse and children)

2. How We Collect Your Information

We collect your information through:

  • Forms you fill out on our website
  • Phone calls or text conversations
  • Emails or other direct communications
  • Optional: future tools such as cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts

3. Use of Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Help connect you with the right agent
  • Provide you with services or products
  • Follow up on requests, questions, or support
  • Improve our services

4. Sharing Your Information

We only share your information with authorized agents working under Family Values Group. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child without verified parental consent, we will delete that information.

6. Your Consent

By submitting your information through our website or other channels, you consent to this privacy policy.

7. Your Rights

You may request to access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us at fvinsuranceagency@gmail.com.

8. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. All changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
